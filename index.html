Our Process

How We Craft Your Momos

At Mom's Kitchen, the owner prepares all dishes in front of you in one of the cleanest setups you will find in Midnapore. Here is how we make it special.

01

Handmade Wheat Dough

We knead the dough fresh multiple times a day using filtered water, with zero additives, bleaching agents, or preservatives.

02

Fresh Minced Fillings

We source local green cabbage, carrots, onions, paneer, and fresh chicken daily. Filled with clean proteins and spices.

03

Steamed in Traditional Bamboo

Steaming in natural organic bamboo baskets gives the momos a delicate moisture and maintains hygienic standards.

04

Fresh Ground Chili Sauce

Grinding dry red chilies, garlic, and vinegar daily to make our signature fiery chutney. Served alongside cooling mint mayo.
