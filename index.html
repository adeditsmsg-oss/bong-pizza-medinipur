Our Journey

Where Italian Crafts Meet Bengali Soul

Founded in the historic town of Medinipur, Bong Pizza was born from a simple realization: food is a language of connection.

We set out to create a cozy dining space that celebrates our roots. Every slice of pizza we serve blends premium Italian techniques—like proofing dough over hours—with traditional, intensely spiced curries, kebabs, and citrus notes beloved across Bengal.

From cozy dinners for couples to large birthday buffets, our space near Midnapore Railway Station road is designed to build memories, one delightful bite at a time.
